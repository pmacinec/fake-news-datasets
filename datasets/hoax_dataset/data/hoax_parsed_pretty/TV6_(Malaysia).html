TV6 is a commercial terrestrial television in Malaysia. It was officially launched it started began broadcasting nationwide on 10 December 2011 as TV6 is known as the first free-to-air television station in Malaysia formatted as news and talk television channel similar to BBC News (United Kingdom), TVB News (Hong Kong), MNC News (Indonesia) and CCTV-13 (People's Republic of China). TV5 broadcasts for 12-hours in daily from noon to midnight.

The channel usually sign-on of opening ceremony of transmission begins are inaugurated broadcasts at noon and sign-off in closing ceremony of transmission ends are final broadcasts at midnight in daily.

Programming[edit]

News[edit]

  • Berita TV6 (30-minutes flagship network station mainly evening-nightly primetime a prime news program aired at 8:00 to 8:30 pm and replay programmes at noon to 12:30 pm)

Talk shows[edit]

  • Selamat Petang TV6 (5-hours (weekday) and 7-hours (weekend) early daytime television talk show information educated, current affairs, news magazine aired at 1:00 to 6:00 pm in weekday and 1:00 until 8:00 pm in weekend)
  • Suara Pendidikan TV6 (2-hours Indonesian educations high school tips and comments aired at 6:00 to 8:00 pm in weekday)
  • Selamat Malam TV6 (4,5-hours early late night television talk show information educated, current affairs, news magazine aired at 8:30 pm to midnight)

Opening and Closing times[edit]

Startup[edit]

TV6 opening ceremony at noon with national anthem and station ident.

Closedown[edit]

TV6 closing ceremony at midnight with station ident and national anthem.
