Prince Asim Majid bin Al Hussein was born on May 17, 1988. He is the son of King Hussein and Queen Noor. He is the 44th generation direct descendant of Prophet Mohammad.

Education[edit]

Prince Asim attended Mrs. Jane Mynors's nursery school in West London, he received his elementary education in Amman at the Amman Baccalaureat School and then attended the schools of St. Mark's and Fay in the United States. He later would followed Brittish Royals Prince William & Prince Harry to the Wetherby School, and later to Ludgrove School in Berkshire. Asim Is Currently Attending Duke Univerity
