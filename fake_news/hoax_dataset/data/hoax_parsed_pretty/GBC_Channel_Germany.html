GBC is a premium cable German television channel.

The channel heavily features American series from the major broadcast networks and premium cable networks. They are broadcast without interruptions with both German and English audio.

History[edit]

GBC Channel has been merged with another 2 stations in January 2014. Both stations were among the Universum Studio GmbH Germany. The first station Sports Channel+US the Channel sent only American sports, the second Channel Comedy Channel never went to programs. In March 2014 GBC got the ok from Premiere, Kabel Deutschland and KabelBW. Since April 2014 GBC sends his program on pay-TV.
